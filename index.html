Welcome! My name is Paul and I am a senior economist at the Federal Reserve Board in Washington, D.C. and also teach Text Mining for Economics and Finance at the University of Maryland.

My research focuses on understanding the role banks play in the real economy. I often incorporate machine learning and natural language processing techniques into my research to quantify unstructured information such as text. Feel free to look at my research page and my tutorials to learn more!
